📘 Lung Health Guide

✅ Do's

  • Exercise regularly — walking, swimming, yoga
  • Stay hydrated — 8 glasses of water daily
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Get annual chest X-rays if high risk
  • Sleep 7–8 hours to support lung recovery

❌ Don'ts

  • Never smoke — including e-cigarettes
  • Avoid exposure to asbestos and dust
  • Don't ignore persistent chest pain
  • Avoid strenuous exercise with chest injury
  • Don't delay seeking care if breathless
  • Avoid high altitude if recently injured

🔍 How to Identify

  • Sudden sharp chest pain on one side
  • Shortness of breath at rest
  • Rapid heart rate (tachycardia)
  • Reduced breath sounds on one side
  • Shoulder or neck pain
  • Bluish skin (cyanosis) in severe cases

💊 Treatment Overview

  • Small cases: observation & rest
  • Needle aspiration to remove air
  • Chest tube insertion for large cases
  • Surgery for recurrent pneumothorax
  • Oxygen therapy to aid reabsorption
  • Pleurodesis to prevent recurrence

🚨 Emergency Warning Signs

😮‍💨

Breathing Difficulty

Cannot complete a full breath, gasping at rest

💙

Bluish Lips or Skin

Cyanosis indicates critically low oxygen levels

❤️

Racing Heart

Heart rate over 120 bpm with chest pain

😵

Loss of Consciousness

Fainting or confusion alongside chest symptoms

⚡ If you experience any of the above — call emergency services immediately
